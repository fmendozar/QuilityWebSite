Overview

Problem

Generally speaking, obsessive-compulsive disorder is a mental illness where intrusive thoughts compel a person to take unnecessary or unproductive actions throughout their day. There are several categories that these actions can fall under but the category that we decided to focus on was the act of repeatedly checking tasks over and over again because this was a common symptom in the users that we were able to contact for our research.

Solution

Quility is a smartwatch application made to combat intrusive thoughts that lead to obsessive checking over completed tasks and serve as a distraction from completing new tasks. Quillity does this by providing the user with confirmation that the task is complete and with therapeutic methods to if the user feels overwhelmed by the intrusive thoughts.
